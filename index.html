The Problem

Standard interview formats screen out capable candidates before they get a fair shot.

Programs serving neurodivergent job seekers see this every cohort: strong candidates who freeze up, mask exhaustively, or get marked down for delivery instead of substance.

Without Adaptive Prep

  • One generic mock-interview format for every candidate
  • Feedback focused on delivery, not on what was actually said
  • No visibility into where a candidate struggles until it's too late
  • Coaching staff time spread thin across large cohorts

With AdaptIQ

  • Coaching adjusts to each candidate's cognitive profile
  • Feedback separates answer content from delivery signals
  • Session-level data shows staff exactly where to focus support
  • Candidates can practice independently, at scale, between sessions
The Product

One continuous loop, running during every practice session.

Perceive, process, and act — in real time, without adding a single manual step for your staff.

01

Perceive

The browser locally reads facial landmarks, eye gaze, head pose, and voice during the session. Nothing raw ever leaves the candidate's device.

02

Process

Lightweight behavioral signals — not video — are reasoned over by Claude to identify confusion, disengagement, or overwhelm as it happens.

03

Act

The candidate gets a real-time, profile-matched intervention. After the session, staff get a structured report — not just a number.

Security & Privacy

Built to survive procurement review, not just a demo.

Video and audio never leave the candidate's device unprocessed. Only abstracted behavioral signals — not footage — are sent to Claude for reasoning.

No raw video storage

Face and voice processing happens client-side. Recordings are never uploaded or retained by default.

Metadata-only reasoning

Only derived signals (gaze deviation, pace, tone) are sent for AI reasoning — never raw sensor data.

Per-organization deployment

Session data and reporting stay scoped to your program — not pooled across unrelated organizations.

Built on Anthropic's Claude

Reasoning is powered by Claude via the Anthropic API, under Anthropic's enterprise data-handling terms.

Questions

Before you sign in.

Does AdaptIQ record or store my video?

No. Face and voice processing happens locally in your browser. Only abstracted behavioral signals — not raw footage — are ever sent anywhere, and only for the purpose of generating real-time coaching.

What do I need to use it?

A modern browser (Chrome recommended), a webcam, and a microphone. No install required — it runs entirely in the browser.

Is it free?

AdaptIQ started as a hackathon project (1st place, Claude NJIT Hackathon 2026) and individual practice sessions are free to use. Program-level plans below are for organizations that want reporting, admin tools, and support at scale.

What happens when I sign in with Google?

Firebase handles authentication — we never see or store your Google password. Once signed in, you're taken straight into the interview practice product.

Which cognitive profiles are supported?

ADHD, Anxiety, and ASD-oriented profiles today, each with different intervention styles and sensitivity thresholds — you can also select specific weaknesses to focus on (eye contact, stuttering, distraction, pacing, anxiety).
